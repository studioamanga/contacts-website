Your contacts are always synchronized, you don’t have to worry about it. The app is directly connected to your iPhone or iPad address book.
It works with iCloud Contacts, Google Contacts, Yahoo Contacts, and any LDAP or CardDAV account.

You can also use custom mail clients, like Sparrow and Gmail.
